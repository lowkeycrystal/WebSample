Suhot Cave and Spring, Capiz


Suhot Cave and Spring Cold water are located in the Municipality of Dumalag, province of Capiz. The famous Suhot Cave and Spring Resort are located in this place. About 300 meters away from the provincial highway.
It is one of the longest caves and magnificent resort that can be found in the country. Suhot Cave and Spring Cold water has its crystal and clear water that came from under the cave, which you can enjoy swimming. They have cottages which are suitable for taking rest after swimming or roaming around.

Bakhawan Eco Park,  Aklan


The 220-hecatre park is actually dubbed as the country’s most successful mangrove reforestation project. And a little fun fact, bakhawan, in the local dialect, actually stands for the word mangrove.
The park is a good destination for tourists who just need to take a breather from busy streets and crowded places. Its 1.3-kilometer long bamboo trail passes by several fish and crustacean farms, and ends with a stunning view of the sea.
