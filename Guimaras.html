All about Guimaras

Guimaras is located in the region of West Visayas Region VI in Iloilo. Its Capital is Jordan and Guimaras is considered as the smallest province in Panay. Guimaras is well known for its sweet and healthy mango fruits. The local municipal of Guimaras held its annual Mangagahan Festival every 20th-22nd of May.

The Fruit Mango

It is considered as the symbol of the Island Guimaras because of their natural local mango that has a different sweetness among other local mango, a lot of peole are known to explore guimaras just to taste the amazing mango from the village.
That's why Guimaras has its festival called Manggahan Festival thas is held annualy. The Manggahan Festival is one of the factor that keeps attracting tourist and help to boost tourism in Guimaras.

Natural Beaches

There are a lot of resort that has a beautiful front beach view also places where you can actual feel the wonderful gift of nature to Guimaras, its beaches isn't taunting as Boracay but its marvelous for its freshness and cuteness because its just a small islan also its sand is just as fine as high class beach in the whole island of the country. Doing island hopping will be fun and exciting but not tiring one.
If you want to experience the lively city life? it is not a problem because Guimaras is just near to Iloilo City where you can enjoy different kind of lifestyle.
